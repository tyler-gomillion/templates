Rules of the Game

Here's a run down of the very basic rules of the game Roblox, developed by Roblox Corp. The game is a multiplayer only game and common problems with multiplayer only games is the amount of toxicity roaming around the community. In order to combat this, please follow these vague but helpful rules.

  1. Be nice even if they aren't being nice to you.
  2. Help new folks to the game.
  3. No matter what, DO NOT swear. This can get you banned instantly.
  4. Keep it PG.
  5. Respect your friends. If you want to play a certain game and they don't want to play with you, don't go to their house and threaten their mother.
  6. Always stay in character if roleplaying.
  7. Avoid toxic players.
  8. Play with friends
  9. While following rule 8, be open to make new friends.
